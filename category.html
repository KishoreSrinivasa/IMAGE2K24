TECHNICAL EVENTS

Image 2k24 • Mar 6th'24

PAPER PRESENTATION

An individual or group of participants may present their topics during the paper presentation in front of the juries. Within 10 minutes, participants should cover their ece-related or emerging technology-related topics. The presentation needs to be in PPTX format.
RULES :
Per team consist of Maximum 3 members.
Background should be white/light colours Preferable
Presentation should consist of maximum 08-12 slides.

Image 2k24 • Mar 6th'24

PROJECT PRESENTATION

Project Presentation is a group event where participants can showcase their unique ideas by presenting a functioning prototype in front of judges and submit a project report.

RULES :
Participants can perform as an individual or a team.
Per team consist of maximum three members.
Participants should bring their working model of the project.

Image 2k24 • Mar 6th'24

CODING PRO'S

Experience the thrill of quick problem-solving at our Coding Challenge Event! Participants aim to crack the code in 30 minutes. Join us for a high-energy challenge that emphasizes quick thinking and efficient coding.

RULES :
Participants should perform only as a individual.

CIRCUIT HUNT

Embark on an electrifying adventure with our 'Experience Circuit Hunt' event! Participants will unravel the mysteries of circuit diagrams, seeking and correcting errors for a seamless connection. Sharpen your problem-solving skills and join us for a hands-on journey through the circuitscape!
RULES :
Participants can perform as an individual.

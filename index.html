PAPER PRESENTATION

An individual or group of participants may present their topics during the paper presentation in front of the juries.

PROJECT PRESENTATION

Project Presentation is a group event where participants can showcase their unique ideas by presenting a functioning prototype in front of judges and submitting a project report.

CODING PRO'S

A fast-paced technical challenge in which competitors demonstrate their coding knowledge by solving complex problems in a 30-minute time period, exhibiting speed, accuracy, and problem-solving abilities.

CIRCUIT HUNT

This technical event pushes participants to discover and correct errors, cultivating skills in diagnosing and debugging electrical systems.

Workshops

Image 2k24 • Mar 6th'24

INTERNET OF THINGS(hands-on)

By industrial experts

The workshop's goal is to equip students with the technical knowledge required to safeguard Internet of Things (IoT) systems and networks. To produce graduates organizing, implementing, and monitoring IoT security precautions that can help secure the integrity and confidentiality of information within IoT technology.

PRE REQUIREMENTS ~

Attendees are recommended to bring individual laptops for hands-on training.
